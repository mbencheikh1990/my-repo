About Me

I guess I should talk about myself! Despite going to a technology-oriented high school, my parents encouraged me to do my major in "Criminal Justice," hoping I would one day become an attorney or something. That never panned out, and due to my ADHD I never really got the most out of secondary or post-secondary education. Despite enjoying the physical and life sciences and being pretty tech-savy, getting into programming didn't really cross my mind during my earlier years. I spent my twenties holding down a bunch of dead-end jobs to support myself, not really knowing what I was going to do with my life. Then I started to learn about coding bootcamps and how they can provide opportunities to those willing to work hard and learn, even if they don't have a degree. I've since been getting into programming and I am really growing to love it! I hope I can do something special with these skills that I am building and I hope the Flatiron School can help me take that next step towards a career as a programming!
